Brief

A smart home system that tries to do the right thing, but is more a nuisance than a help.

Description

The punishing home is a smart home project that aims to let people see and experience how much they rely on technology in their daily lives. in this project we made a smart home system that tries to give the ideal environment, but annoys the person and gets in their way.

This rubs against the ethical implications of AI and smart home technology.

The project is build from the ground up and is a amalgamation of various technologies including sensors, actuators, machine learning, speech to text and python.

The project is made by 5 students of which consisted of 3 creative technologist and 2 AI engineers.

Goal

Let users experience an extreme situation where the smart home system chooses what is best for them, but in a way that is annoying and frustrating.

Context

For the studio named Dark Tech we got the assignment named Smart Home: Punishing Home, this meant we had to make a product that simulates a smart home setting. but we had to look at how much damage it would cause if, the home system wouldn't help you but force you to listen to his commands or else it would sabotage you.
